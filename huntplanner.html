Legal Sunrise & Hunt Planner

This tool is designed to calculate legal sunrise based on the NGPC published sunrise table in the 2025-2026 Small Game Guide. You can find the guide here. This planner also allows you to reverse plan your timeline for the hunt and easily share it with friends. All times are in central.

Select the date for your hunting trip. This calculator works between 9/1/2025 and 2/28/2026.
Total drive time, including buffer time for delays.

Time to set up your blind, decoys, or stand for hunting.
